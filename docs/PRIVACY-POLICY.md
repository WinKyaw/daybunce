## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

DayBunce is designed with privacy in mind. All data is stored **locally on your device** and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties. The App collects and stores the following information locally:

- **Inventory Items**: Names, prices, quantities, categories, and unit types you enter
- **Daily Records**: Date-stamped inventory information you create
- **Custom Categories**: Categories you create for organizing items
- **Custom Unit Types**: Unit types you define for measurements
- **Language Preferences**: Your selected language and custom translations
- **Images**: Photos taken or selected for OCR processing (processed locally only)

### Information Automatically Collected

The App may automatically collect certain technical information to function properly:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version (for compatibility)
- **Storage Information**: Local storage usage (to manage data and cleanup)
- **App Usage**: Basic app functionality data (stored locally for app operation)
- **File Timestamps**: For data management and automatic cleanup of old records

**Important**: This information is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

## How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively for the following purposes:

1. **App Functionality**: To provide core inventory management features
2. **Data Persistence**: To save and retrieve your inventory records locally
3. **OCR Processing**: To extract text from images you capture (processed locally only)
4. **Language Support**: To display the app in your preferred language
5. **Data Management**: To automatically clean up data older than 30 days
6. **User Experience**: To remember your preferences and settings

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage Only

DayBunce operates entirely offline with **100% local storage**:

- All data is stored using AsyncStorage on your device
- No data is transmitted to external servers or cloud services
- No internet connection is required for the App to function
- Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it

### Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your locally stored data:

- Data is stored using secure device storage mechanisms
- Camera and storage permissions are requested only when needed
- Images are processed locally and can be deleted after use
- No analytics or tracking services are embedded in the App

### Data Retention

- Daily inventory records are automatically cleaned up after 30 days
- You can manually delete any data at any time
- Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data
- You have full control over your data export and import
